Glossary

  • Thug Life: Expression popularized by American rapper Tupac Shakur and which became viral on the web via the hashtag #THUGLIFE. Often misinterpreted, it does not glorify violence, but rather expresses the desire to elevate the soul. Thug Life is an acronym for “The Hate U Give Little Infants Fucks Everyone,” meaning “The Hate You Give to Children Destroys Everyone.”

  • Ghetto: Originally, a neighborhood imposed on Jews by the authorities, confining them to live separately from the non-Jewish population. This concept allowed Jews to practice Judaism and organize while remaining under state control. Although the ghetto maintains cultural specificities, it does not imply real autonomy. In a more contemporary context, the term "ghetto" refers to a deprived and densely populated urban neighborhood, often inhabited by ethnic minorities.

  • Acronym: A word formed by taking the first letters of a phrase or series of words. Acronyms are often used to simplify and remember complex expressions.

  • Ethnic minorities: Groups of people sharing a common ethnic origin and constituting a relatively small proportion of the total population of a region or country.
