Chronology

Ready to break through

Publication : January 2, 2020

Author : Angie Thomas

Genres : Young Adult Literature, Coming-of-Life Novel, Domestic Fiction

Bri is sixteen years old and dreams of being the greatest rapper of all time. Or at least win your first battle. She has someone to take after her - her father was a rap legend, until he was killed by the gangs. But when her mother loses her job, their landlord threatens to throw them out and violence increases in her neighborhood, Bri no longer has a choice: succeeding in rap is no longer a dream. It is a necessity.

Concrete Pink

Publication : January 6, 2022

Author : Angie Thomas

Genres :Coming-of-age novel, Domestic fiction

Maverick, a high school senior in 1998 Garden Heights, is about to follow in the footsteps of his father, a prison drug lord. Everything changes when he discovers he is a father himself and struggles to raise his baby while seeking to escape gang life.

Blackout in New York

Publication :June 9, 2022

Authors : Tiffany D.Jackson, Dhonielle Clayton, Nicola Yoon, Angie Thomas

contributor : collective

Gender : Love novel

When the lights go out, hearts light up... A scorching summer in New York - and now a gigantic power outage plunges the entire city into darkness. But while confusion reigns, another kind of sparks illuminates the darkness... Strangers that chance brings together. Long-time friends. Exes forced to live together. In the dark, there is no point in cheating, everyone can reveal their truth, their feelings. Love is exposed, friendship is transformed. Tammi, JJ, Nella, Lana, Kayla, Seymour... From Manhattan to Brooklyn, the footsteps of these black teenagers cross, move away and rejoin in the blocked city, shining like so many flames.
