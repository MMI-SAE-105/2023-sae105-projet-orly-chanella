Emotional and contemporary partition

Original music composed by Dustin O’Halloran

Music plays a vital role in creating the atmosphere of a film, and in “The Hate U Give” it is a powerful component that accompanies the story from start to finish. The film's soundtrack is the collaboration of two talented composers, Dustin O'Halloran and Mark Isham, who successfully capture the complex emotions and deep themes of the story.

Dustin O’Halloran, music composer, was chosen by George Tillman Jr to work on the soundtrack of the film “The Hate U Give”

George had also collaborated with Mark Isham, who created piano-based music and powerful violins for the film. The music in the film shifts from more traditional compositions to more contemporary electronic sounds to accommodate the emotional evolution of the main character.

Modular synthesizers are used to create a modern atmosphere, while also connecting with the many hip-hop songs featured in the film.
