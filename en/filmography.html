BIG GEORGE FOREMAN

George Foreman, who had won an Olympic gold medal, entered the ring for a historic fight against Mohamed Ali in Zaire. He later returned to boxing at the age of 45 to become the oldest heavyweight champion of all time.

CROSSED PATHS

Sophia, a brilliant student, meets Luke at a rodeo competition, despite their differences. On the other side, 90-year-old Ira Levinson narrowly escapes an accident thanks to the help of Luke and Sophia. Ira begs them to save a box of precious letters. Sophia offers to read them to Ira at the hospital, discovering his story and striking similarities to her own life alongside Luke. Despite the years that separate them, they share similar experiences and the hope of a happy life.

FASTER

Upon his release from prison, the Driver has only one idea in mind: to avenge the murder of his brother, killed during the robbery which went wrong and sent him behind bars for ten years. Having become a free man again, he can finally devote himself to the missions which he has made a precise list...

NOTORIOUS B.I.G.

Notorious B.I.G. follows the remarkable rise of Christopher Wallace, who, in a few months, will go from the mean streets of Brooklyn to the status of hip-hop legend.
