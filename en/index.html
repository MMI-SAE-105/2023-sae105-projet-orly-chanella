The positive impact in the literature contemporary

Angie Thomas succeeded in creating a narrative who left an indelible mark in contemporary literature and which will continue to inspire readers to think and act.

The courage to speak out against injustice

Starr managed to go out into her comfort zone and spoke in front of her comrades, she managed to convey her anger, her frustration, her determination and decided to speak out against injustice, she was a figure of inspiration for those who knew how to listen to it.

The positive impact of the film adaptation

The adaptation of this novel to the screen was a great success, the director managed to stay true to the themes of the book, exploring police brutality, racial injustice and identity, this allowed the film to retain the emotional impact of the novel.

The musical touch

Music plays a vital role in creating the atmosphere of a film, and in “The Hate U Give” it is a powerful component that accompanies the story from start to finish.
